Summary:

  • Without Container:
    Element(s) will spread with just a little padding on its left-right side.
  • With Container:
    Element(s) will be centered with relative width depending on its viewport's width size.
  • Without Container:
    Element(s) will have the same width just like Without Container. But with a little bit added-padding on its left-right side.
